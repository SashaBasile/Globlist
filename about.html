About

What is Globlist?

Globlist is an interactive city lists. Why? Have you ever wanted to make sure not to miss the best spots in the city? Perhaps share it with your friends or create your own personal check-in-lists? Then, Globlist is for you.
Explore your city's map and save all the spots you really don't want to miss!
